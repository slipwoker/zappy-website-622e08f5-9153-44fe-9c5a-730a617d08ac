    Our Story & Mission

    Swift Restoration was founded with a clear mission: to provide property management companies in the San Francisco Bay Area with reliable, professional restoration and remediation services they can count on. We recognized that property managers needed more than just emergency response—they needed a true partner who understands their unique challenges and priorities.

    As one of the leading mold remediation companies in the region, we've built our reputation on rapid response, thorough work, and transparent communication. Every member of our team is trained to think like a property manager, understanding that minimizing tenant disruption, maintaining detailed documentation, and working efficiently are just as important as technical expertise.

    Today, we serve hundreds of properties across the Bay Area, from residential complexes to commercial buildings. Our success comes from treating every property as if it were our own and every property manager as a valued partner. We don't just restore buildings—we restore peace of mind.
